SATURDAY NIGHT.

Two friend venture to the edge of their town on a dark night. They walk upto a single story house made of dull colored wood, entering through the creaking front door. The door locks behind them as they try to scare one another as a joke. Help them find something in the house so they can open the door and escape.

They must pick a room to begin their search for a way out.
